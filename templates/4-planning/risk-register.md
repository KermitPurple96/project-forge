## Risk categories to consider

- **Technical:** Scaling issues, API limits, framework breaking changes
- **Security:** Data breaches, auth bypass, injection attacks
- **Business:** Competitor launches, regulation changes, funding
- **Team:** Key person leaves, skill gaps, burnout
- **Timeline:** Scope creep, dependencies delayed, underestimated complexity
